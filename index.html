General pathway to transition into software development

Introduction to Software Development

Use various online tutorials and bootcamps to learn the concepts of different programming languages and the range of ways they can be applied in practical situations.

Gain Experience

Through a combination of personal projects, practice exercises from bootcamps and contributing to open-source software, build up your experience by expanding your portfolio of work.

Monetization

Take your first steps to turning this hobby into a career by completing projects for clients as a freelance software developer. Build an online presence and continue doing jobs for clients to continuously add to your portfolio.

Career Goals

When you believe you have enough experience and you are ready to take the next step, either increase your capacity as a freelance software developer by devoting more hours per day to completing projects for clients or begin applying for software development roles in professional organisations and work from there.
